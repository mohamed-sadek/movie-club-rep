Club Rules

  • Rule #1: The movie has to be unseen by the both parties.
  • Rule #2: Only black & white movies permitted.
  • Rule #3: No movie after the year 1970.
  • Rule #4: The movie should not exceed a duration of 135 min.
  • Rule #5: Exceptions may apply, certain movies beyond the year 1970 are permissable, maximum 1973.
  • Rule #6: In the case of a boring movie, it could be terminated after 30 minutes.
  • Rule #7: Sleeping during the movie is forbidden.
